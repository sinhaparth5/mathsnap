## Variant 1: Product Launch

I built `mathsnap` to make LaTeX math rendering much easier in frontend apps.

It works across React, Vue, Svelte, and even vanilla JS/TS, so you do not need separate setups for every stack. It wraps KaTeX in a simple API, includes TypeScript support, ships with optimized CSS, and adds built-in error handling so bad equations do not break your UI.

If you need to render clean math equations without spending time wiring everything together manually, this package is for you.

https://www.npmjs.com/package/mathsnap
